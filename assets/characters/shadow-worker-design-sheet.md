# Shadow Worker — Character Design Sheet

_The 'Raised' subordinates of Juan dela Cruz. Employees who broke under corporate pressure, now resurrected as ethereal, glitchy shadows bound to the Work System. Anime-style character design sheet format._

**UNIVERSAL DESIGN SPECS:**

- **Background:** Transparent (MUST be transparent)
- **Background Alternative:** Solid WHITE background
- **Character Only:** Full-body character isolation
- **Style:** Anime character design sheet aesthetic. Solo Leveling shadow soldier visual language (dark blues, deep purples, flickering energy). 
- **Core Visual:** A silhouette of a standard office worker, but made of shifting, glitchy shadow energy. Instead of fire or smoke, they emit "digital smoke"—pixels and scanning lines that dissipate into the air.
- **Clothing:** Distorted versions of office wear (suits, lanyards, ties) that seem part of their shadow body. Ties might look like flickering energy tails.
- **Face:** Featureless except for glowing "Corporate Blue" eyes. No mouths, no noses.
- **Key Detail:** A flickering, translucent ID lanyard that glows with the worker's original Rank (F, D, etc.).
- **Mood Across All Views:** Eerie, efficient, silent, and loyal. They move with unnatural precision.
