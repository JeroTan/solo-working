# Character Art Prompt — Benny

---

## Character Overview

**Name:** Benny
**Role:** Senior Developer (Rank B)
**Novel:** Solo Working

---

## Prompt

Character illustration for "Solo Working," a Filipino office parody manhwa-style novel. The character is Benny — Senior Developer, Rank B. He is the guardian of the legacy COBOL systems, a man who views any new code as a personal insult.

**Physical appearance:** Filipino male, late 30s. He has the unpolished look of a man who hasn't seen the sun in days. His hair is messy and unkempt. His eyes are narrowed and cynical behind thick glasses that reflect the cold blue light of multiple monitors.

**Clothing:** A faded navy blue polo shirt from a company event five years ago, slightly too large. Loose khaki trousers. His ID lanyard is tattered and covered in old security stickers, a badge of his tenure and his resistance to change.

**Posture:** Defensive and hunched. He stands with his arms crossed or leans over his keyboard like a dragon guarding its hoard. He occupies his space with a prickly, hostile energy that keeps juniors at a distance.

**Expression:** A permanent scowl of technical arrogance. He looks like he’s in the middle of a "well, actually" explanation. He has the "Toxic Rank: B" signature of someone who is competent but makes everyone else's job harder.

**Lighting:** Cold, flickering blue light from server racks and multiple monitors. Harsh shadows that emphasize the tired lines on his face and the unpolished texture of his clothes.

**Background:** A cluttered server room or a dark corner of the office. Tangled cables, old hardware, and a "NO ENTRY" sign on a server rack in the background.

**Mood:** Hostile gatekeeping. Technical debt as a weapon. The "Elite Grunt" who serves as a mid-boss obstacle.

**Style:** Cinematic manhwa illustration. High contrast. Filipino facial features. He should look like the veteran developer you’re afraid to ask for help from.

**No text. No watermarks. No logos.**
