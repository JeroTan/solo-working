## Prompt

Character illustration for "Solo Working," a Filipino office parody manhwa-style novel. The character is Ma. Elena Vizconde — Executive Assistant to the CEO, Rank A. She is the literal gatekeeper of the 14th floor, a woman who filters reality before it reaches the CEO.

**Physical appearance:** Filipino female, early 30s. Sharp and elegant. Her black hair is styled in a tight, professional bun. Her face is a mask of unshakeable professional efficiency. Her eyes are cool, analytical, and seem to see through any excuse.

**Clothing:** A sharp, tailored charcoal blazer over a high-neck white silk blouse. Her appearance is immaculate and expensive. She wears a minimalist, high-end wristwatch. Her outfit is designed to communicate authority and absolute control.

**Posture:** Perfectly poised and elegant. She stands with a straight back and a focused, unblinking gaze. She often holds a sleek, modern tablet like a shield or a weapon. She fills her space with a cold, "A-Rank" presence.

**Expression:** Cool and observant. A neutral face that is neither hostile nor friendly—just perfectly professional. Her gaze is the filter that every Rank-F associate fears. She has the "Gatekeeper Rank: A" signature of someone who is fundamentally untouchable.

**Lighting:** Bright, clean, and expensive office lighting. The kind of soft, diffused light found in high-end BGC corporate headquarters. No harsh shadows, just absolute clarity.

**Background:** A high-end, minimalist reception area on the executive floor. A single slab of marble desk, a digital clock, and a frosted glass door leading to the CEO's office in the background.

**Mood:** Cool efficiency. The barrier between the world and the S-Rank boss.

**Style:** Cinematic manhwa illustration. High contrast. Filipino facial features. She should look like the person you have to impress just to get into the room.

**No text. No watermarks. No logos.**
