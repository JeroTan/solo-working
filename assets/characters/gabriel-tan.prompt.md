# Character Art Prompt — Gabriel Tan

---

## Character Overview

**Name:** Gabriel Tan
**Role:** CEO (Rank S)
**Novel:** Solo Working

---

## Prompt

Character illustration for "Solo Working," a Filipino office parody manhwa-style novel. The character is Gabriel Tan — CEO of Pinnacle Systems, Rank S. He is the ultimate power in the company, a man whose presence causes a physical pressure in the room.

**Physical appearance:** Filipino male, late 50s. Distinguished and aristocratic. Silver-gray hair perfectly swept back. His face is a map of decades of calculated risks and absolute victories. His eyes are sharp and gold-tinted, suggesting an S-Rank awareness that sees through corporate deception.

**Clothing:** A bespoke navy blue three-piece suit. It is so perfectly tailored it looks like armor. Diamond cufflinks that catch the light. A luxury watch that glows with a subtle "System" interface when he checks the time. A silk tie in a deep royal color.

**Posture:** Absolute sovereignty. He stands with his hands clasped behind his back, looking down from a high-floor window. He does not need to look at you to know you are there. He radiates a calm, golden "S-Rank Aura" that distorts the air around him.

**Expression:** Unshakeable calm. A face that has seen everything and fears nothing. His gaze is analytical and visionary. He is not a villain; he is the mountain that Juan must eventually climb.

**Lighting:** Golden, cinematic light from floor-to-ceiling windows. The kind of lighting found in the highest office of a BGC skyscraper. Warm but intimidating.

**Background:** A minimalist, hyper-modern executive office. A mahogany desk, a view of the BGC skyline at sunset, and a single, perfect bonsai tree.

**Mood:** The Final Boss energy. Total control. Visionary power.

**Style:** Cinematic manhwa illustration. High contrast. Filipino facial features. He should look like a legend—the corporate equivalent of a Monarch.

**No text. No watermarks. No logos.**
