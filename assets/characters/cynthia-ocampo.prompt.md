## Character Overview

**Name:** Cynthia Ocampo
**Role:** Second Shadow Worker — Presentation & Client Communication Specialist
**Novel:** Solo Working (Filipino office parody of Solo Leveling)

## Prompt

Character illustration for "Solo Working," a Filipino office parody manhwa-style novel. The character is Cynthia Ocampo — the Second Shadow Worker, a former star presenter who was sidelined and forgotten, later Raised by the protagonist.

**Physical appearance:** Filipino woman, early-to-mid 30s. Poised, composed, with the precise bearing of someone who has stood in front of difficult clients and made them believe things. She was once the most polished person in the room without trying. She still is — but now there is nothing warm underneath it. The grace has become architecture.

**Pre-Raise appearance (optional alternate):** Seated at her desk, not working. A presentation deck is open on her screen — a project she built, with someone else's name on the title slide. Her hands are folded in her lap. She is looking at the screen. Her expression is nothing. Not anger. Not sadness. *Nothing.* This is what breaking looks like in someone who was too professional to cry at work.

**Post-Raise appearance (primary):** Standing. Straight. She holds a tablet in one hand displaying a slide deck mid-edit — her edits, visible as red annotations. Her other hand is at her side, fingers slightly curled, as though she is restraining the impulse to fix everything in the room simultaneously. Her eyes scan every surface she passes for design flaws.

**Clothing (Post-Raise):** A tailored blazer — dark navy or charcoal — over a simple blouse. Pressed trousers. Professional to a degree that has become slightly unnerving. Nothing out of place. Not a wrinkle. Not a stray thread. She is, like her slides, pixel-perfect.

**Lighting:** Warm projector light from somewhere ahead — the light of a presentation in progress. It illuminates her face from the front, creating a theatrical quality. The fluorescent office light above creates a cold rim light on her shoulders and the top of her head.

**Background:** Optional dark conference room, a presentation slide projected on a screen behind her (abstract, no readable text), or pure black.

**Mood:** Lethal polish. She will rebuild your entire slide deck from scratch if Slide 7 is weak, and she will tell you about it the way a surgeon mentions an incision: matter-of-fact, without cruelty, and without any doubt whatsoever. She is not unkind. She simply has standards that most people cannot reach.

**Style:** Cinematic manhwa illustration. High contrast lines. Filipino facial features. Post-Raise Shadow Worker aesthetic — elegance so precise it reads as menace.

**No text. No watermarks. No logos.**
