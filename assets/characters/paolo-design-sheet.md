# Paolo — Character Design Sheet

_Sales Associate. Rank-D (True Rank: F). The 'Time-Waster' debuff personified. A man who knows every rumor in the office but none of his own KPIs. Anime-style character design sheet format._

**UNIVERSAL DESIGN SPECS:**

- **Background:** Transparent
- **Background Alternative:** Solid WHITE
- **Character Only:** Full-body character isolation
- **Skin Tone:** Warm Filipino tan
- **Style:** Anime character design sheet aesthetic. Relaxed and expressive. Solo Leveling aesthetic applied to a "mob" character.
- **Clothing:** Casual office wear—a polo shirt with a loud pattern or a bright color, dark jeans or chinos, and trendy sneakers. His ID lanyard is usually tucked into a pocket or hanging loosely.
- **Hair:** Black, styled with a bit too much gel, maybe a trendy "undercut."
- **Face:** Friendly, expressive Filipino features. A wide, easy smile and eyes that are always looking for the next conversation.
- **Build:** Average build, very relaxed posture.
- **Key Detail:** Often holding a coffee cup or a smartphone.
- **Mood Across All Views:** High-energy, distracting, and cheerful. He is the walking personification of a productivity drain.
