# Character Art Prompt — Shadow Worker

---

## Character Overview

**Name:** Shadow Worker
**Role:** Summons / Minions
**Novel:** Solo Working

---

## Prompt

Character illustration for "Solo Working," a Filipino office parody manhwa-style novel. The entity is a "Shadow Worker" — a corporate employee who has been "Raised" by Juan dela Cruz. These are the corporate equivalents of Solo Leveling's Shadow Soldiers.

**Physical appearance:** A humanoid silhouette composed of swirling dark blue and deep violet energy. The form is distinctly that of a corporate employee in a suit, but without any physical features. The only visible features are two sharp, glowing neon-blue eyes that cut through the darkness.

**Clothing:** The ethereal, flickering shape of a standard business suit, tie, and dress shoes. A glowing, translucent ID lanyard hangs from their neck, displaying their original Rank in a ghostly blue font.

**Posture:** Perfectly rigid and robotic. They stand with an unnatural stillness, hands at their sides or typed on an ethereal keyboard. They do not breathe; they only exist to follow orders.

**Expression:** Featureless and silent. The glowing blue eyes show absolute obedience and a machine-like focus.

**Lighting:** Dark, high-contrast lighting. The only light source is the glowing blue eyes of the shadows and the "digital smoke" (pixels and data lines) that rise from their bodies like embers.

**Background:** A dark, empty office at night. The flickering screens of idle workstations, rows of empty cubicles, or the cold metal of a server rack.

**Mood:** Eerie, loyal, and efficient. The "Army of the Overworked" resurrected into a perfect, silent labor force.

**Style:** Cinematic manhwa illustration. High contrast. Sharp, jagged energy lines similar to the shadow soldiers in Solo Leveling.

**No text. No watermarks. No logos.**

## Variant Prompts

**Variant A — The Raising:**
Juan dela Cruz standing in the center of a cubicle floor, arm outstretched. Behind him, dozens of Shadow Workers are rising from the floor like smoke, their glowing blue eyes igniting one by one. The shadows are beginning to type on invisible keyboards, their hands moving with blurring speed.

**Variant B — The Silent Audit:**
A single Shadow Worker standing directly behind a terrified Aldrich Santos. The shadow is holding an ethereal tablet, its glowing eyes fixed on Aldrich's screen. Aldrich is sweating, unaware that he's being audited by a ghost.
