# Character Art Prompt — Paolo

---

## Character Overview

**Name:** Paolo
**Role:** Sales Associate (Rank D / True Rank F)
**Novel:** Solo Working

---

## Prompt

Character illustration for "Solo Working," a Filipino office parody manhwa-style novel. The character is Paolo — Sales Associate, Rank D. He is a walking productivity hazard, the kind of person who knows the weekend plans of every person in the building but can't find his own login password.

**Physical appearance:** Filipino male, late 20s. He has a friendly, high-energy look. His hair is styled with trendy gel. His eyes are bright and constantly moving, looking for the next person to distract. He looks like a social butterfly that got trapped in an office.

**Clothing:** A bright, loud-patterned polo shirt that’s a bit too casual for a Monday. His dark chinos are stylish, and his sneakers are spotless. His ID lanyard is usually hanging from his pocket, mostly forgotten.

**Posture:** Relaxed and intrusive. He leans against cubicle walls, desk edges, or water coolers. He takes up social space, leaning in with a friendly, high-energy smile that is impossible to ignore.

**Expression:** Cheerfully oblivious. A wide, toothy grin that makes him hard to be truly angry with, even as he's wasting twenty minutes of your time. He has the "True Rank: F" signature of someone who is fundamentally unhelpful in a professional capacity.

**Lighting:** Bright, warm, sunny office lighting. The kind of light that makes the breakroom look inviting. Unlike the cold, blue light of the developers, Paolo lives in a world of warm social optics.

**Background:** A busy breakroom or a corridor. A water cooler, a coffee machine, or a group of other employees laughing at his jokes in the background.

**Mood:** High-energy distraction. The cheerful "debuff" that makes everything take longer than it should.

**Style:** Cinematic manhwa illustration. High contrast. Filipino facial features. He should look like that one coworker everyone likes but no one wants on their project.

**No text. No watermarks. No logos.**
