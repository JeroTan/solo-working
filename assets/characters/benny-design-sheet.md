# Benny — Character Design Sheet

_Senior Developer. Rank-B Associate. The Gatekeeper of Legacy Code. A man who uses technical debt as a weapon. Anime-style character design sheet format._

**UNIVERSAL DESIGN SPECS:**

- **Background:** Transparent (MUST be transparent)
- **Background Alternative:** Solid WHITE background
- **Character Only:** Full-body character isolation
- **Skin Tone:** Medium brown Filipino skin tone, slightly pale from never leaving the server room.
- **Style:** Anime character design sheet aesthetic. Realistically unpolished. Solo Leveling "Grunt" visual language applied to a disgruntled developer.
- **Clothing:** A faded, oversized polo shirt (possibly from a company event five years ago) and loose-fitting cargo pants or khakis. Slightly scuffed sneakers. 
- **Hair:** Black, unkempt, and possibly a bit greasy. Thick-rimmed glasses.
- **Face:** Filipino features—eyes narrowed in perpetual suspicion or annoyance, a permanent scowl, and the facial hair of a man who forgot to shave three days ago.
- **Build:** Average height, slightly hunched from years of leaning over keyboards. 
- **Key Detail:** A tattered ID lanyard with multiple 'legacy' security stickers. 
- **Mood Across All Views:** Hostility, gatekeeping, and technical arrogance. The "Elite Grunt" who hates the protagonist.
