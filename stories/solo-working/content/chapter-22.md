# Chapter 22 — The Singapore Hub

*In Manila, the System is a hidden layer, a glitch in the fluorescent lights. In Singapore, the System is the architecture. It’s the air conditioning, the public transport, and the very rhythm of the streets. It’s not just an office anymore. It’s a city-sized dungeon.*

---

The descent into Changi Airport was the first time Juan dela Cruz realized the true scale of what he was dealing with. 

As the plane broke through the clouds, he didn't just see a tropical island. He saw a network of glowing blue lines that mapped the entire city-state. Through his **[ HYBRID CONSCIOUSNESS ]**, the city looked like a massive, three-dimensional motherboard.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               ZONE UPDATE                ║
║                                          ║
║  Environment: THE SINGAPORE HUB          ║
║  Hazard Level: SSS (System Density)      ║
║  Status: [ GUEST WORKER ]                ║
║                                          ║
║  System Integration: 98%                 ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Juan, Gabriel, and Isabel stepped off the plane. They weren't met by customs officers. They were met by a line of kiosks that scanned their ID badges before they even reached the terminal.

"Welcome back, Gabriel," a synthesized voice said from the terminal. "Welcome, Mr. Dela Cruz. Ms. Reyes. Your transport is waiting at Gate S-1."

They walked through the airport, which felt more like a high-end data center than a travel hub. Every surface was clean, every person moved with a purpose, and every screen showed real-time efficiency metrics for the city’s resources.

"It's beautiful," Isabel whispered, her tablet already recording the data flows. "But it's... oppressive. I can't find a single 'Anomaly' in the entire building."

"That’s because here, anomalies are deleted before they can even be documented," Gabriel said, his voice grim. 

A sleek, black autonomous vehicle was waiting for them. It had no driver, and the windows were tinted so heavily they were like mirrors. 

"Destination: The Founders Summit," the car said as the doors slid shut. 

As they drove through the city, Juan watched the people on the sidewalks. Above every head was a Rank. Not just 'F' or 'E', but a complex hierarchy of labels: **[ RANK B - LOGISTICS ]**, **[ RANK A - URBAN PLANNING ]**, **[ RANK S - COMPLIANCE ]**.

The entire population was part of the System. 

"Rodel," Juan whispered.

The shadow emerged, its dark form flickering against the leather seats. *"Boss, the density here is... intense. It’s like trying to breathe in a server room."*

"Can you find the Founders' network?"

*"I’m trying. But the firewalls are layered. It’s not just code. It’s physical. The city’s power grid is part of the encryption."*

The car slowed as they reached the Marina Bay Sands—three massive towers connected by a ship-like structure on top. But they didn't go to the hotel lobby. The car turned into a private tunnel that led deep beneath the building.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               DUNGEON ENTRANCE           ║
║                                          ║
║  Environment: THE FOUNDERS' CORE         ║
║  Objective: SURVIVE THE SUMMIT.          ║
║  Status: [ UNAUTHORIZED SHADOWS DETECTED ]║
║                                          ║
║  Penalty: IMMEDIATE SYSTEM PURGE         ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

"Rodel! Cynthia! Hide!" Juan commanded.

The shadows vanished just as the car doors opened. 

They were in a massive, underground chamber. The walls were made of white glass, and the floor was a single, glowing screen that showed a map of the world. In the center of the room was a table where twelve people sat. 

One of them was Marcus Vane. 

The other eleven were people Juan had only seen in business magazines—the CEOs of the world’s largest tech companies, the heads of global banks, and the architects of international law. 

They were 'The Founders'.

"Gabriel Soriano," one of the women at the table said. Her Rank was **[ RANK SSS+ - FOUNDER ]**. "You’ve brought the anomaly."

Gabriel stepped forward, his head held high. "I’ve brought the Technical Manager of Project Phoenix. The man who saved my company and exposed your 'Loyalty Bonuses'."

The woman smiled. "We know what he did, Gabriel. We’re the ones who designed the Skyfall logs. We wanted to see who would find them."

She looked at Juan. Her eyes weren't blue. They were gold.

"Juan dela Cruz," she said. "You're not a glitch. You're a feature. And it’s time we showed you what you were really built for."

```
║  ALERT: DETECTION METER: 100% [||||||||||] ║
║  Marcus Vane has activated [ THE PURGE ].║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

The room went dark. Juan felt a massive surge of energy—a System Reset command that hit him like a physical wave. He felt his shadows being pulled away, his Rank being stripped, and his consciousness being deleted.

But then, he felt something else. A spark in his notebook. The one he had left in Manila. 

The Skyfall logs weren't just data. They were a backup.

"I don't think so," Juan whispered, his eyes glowing corporate blue in the darkness. "I’ve already documented this move."
