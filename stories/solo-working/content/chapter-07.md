# Chapter 7 — The Audit Begins

*In a corporation, the truth is just a variable that hasn't been properly adjusted yet. But once you have the logs, the truth becomes a weapon. And weapons require a high enough Rank to wield.*

---

Juan dela Cruz made it back to the 11th floor at 4:22 PM. 

His STAMINA was at a critical 12%. Every step felt like he was wading through thick mud, and the fluorescent lights were starting to pulse with a headache-inducing rhythm. 

He sat at his desk and pulled the black-bound Offshore Ledger from his bag. Beside it, tucked into his notebook, were the Skyfall Logs.

The moment he touched the logs, the System went haywire.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║              ⚠ SYSTEM UPDATE ⚠             ║
║                                          ║
║  Unauthorized data detected.             ║
║  Analyzing 'The Skyfall Logs'...         ║
║  ......................................  ║
║                                          ║
║  [ ANALYSIS COMPLETE ]                   ║
║  New Sub-System Unlocked:                ║
║  [ THE CORRUPTION TRACKER ]              ║
║                                          ║
║  Effect: You can now see the 'True Rank'  ║
║  of employees based on their competence,  ║
║  not their political connections.        ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Juan looked around the office. 

Suddenly, the familiar Ranks above everyone's heads began to flicker and change. 

Paolo, the 'Rank-D' Sales guy, now had a flickering gray **[ TRUE RANK: F ]** hovering over him. Dennis Soriano, the 'Rank-C' Manager, showed a solid **[ TRUE RANK: B ]**—the man was far more competent than he let on, he was just tired.

And then there was Aldrich Santos. 

Aldrich was walking through the floor, heading toward Juan's desk. His official Rank was S. But above his head, the System was flashing a vibrant, angry red:

```
║  NAME: ALDRICH SANTOS                    ║
║  OFFICIAL RANK: S                        ║
║  TRUE RANK: D (INCOMPETENT)              ║
║  STATUS: [ FRAUDULENT ]                  ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

"Juan," Aldrich said, his voice carrying across the quiet floor. "You look terrible. Even for a Rank-F."

He stopped at Juan's desk, looking at the ledger. He didn't see the logs. To him, Juan was just a exhausted employee about to fail.

"I see you retrieved the offshore ledger," Aldrich noted, his eyes narrowing. "How? Elena Vizconde doesn't even give her own boss the key without a form."

"I explained the dependency, sir," Juan said, his voice rasping. "She understood."

"I’m sure she did," Aldrich sneered. "Well, you have less than twenty-four hours. The audit starts at 9:00 AM tomorrow. If those numbers aren't perfectly aligned with the migration data, the failure is yours. Do you understand?"

"I understand, sir."

Aldrich walked away, his 'True Rank: D' flickering like a dying lightbulb. 

Juan waited until he was gone, then whispered, "Rodel. Final push. Process the offshore data. Cross-reference it with the Skyfall logs. I want to know exactly where the money went during the crash."

The shadow emerged, more solid than ever before. 

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║              SHADOW UPGRADE              ║
║                                          ║
║  Because of the [ SKYFALL LOGS ],        ║
║  RODEL has regained a piece of his soul. ║
║                                          ║
║  RODEL Status: [ RECOVERED ]             ║
║  New Skill: [ DATA FORENSICS ]           ║
║  (Can track hidden transactions)         ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

The Shadow Rodel didn't just type now. It *hunted*. 

On the screen, the spreadsheet began to reorganize itself into something Juan hadn't expected. It wasn't just a Q3 report. It was a map of corporate theft. 

During the Skyfall migration, while the servers were melting and the Rank-F workers were breaking, millions of pesos had been moved into a series of "Offshore Maintenance" accounts. Accounts that were managed by Aldrich Santos.

*Bip. Bip. Bip.*

**[ DEADLINE SENSE: 14:00:00 ]**

"Boss," Rodel’s voice was no longer a whisper. It was a clear, calm tone in Juan’s mind. *"The report is ready. But if you submit this version, Aldrich Santos will be fired. And he will take you down with him."*

Juan looked at the screen. The accuracy was 99.8%. The dungeon objective was complete. 

But he had a choice. 

He could submit the 'Official' report—the one Aldrich expected, with the errors conveniently hidden. Or he could submit the 'True' report.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║              ⚠ FINAL CHOICE ⚠            ║
║                                          ║
║  [ THE CORPORATE PATH ]                  ║
║  Submit the edited report.               ║
║  Reward: Rank Up, Safety, Aldrich's Favor.║
║                                          ║
║  [ THE WORKER'S PATH ]                   ║
║  Submit the true report.                 ║
║  Reward: Rank Up, Massive Exp, War.       ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Juan looked at his notebook. At the seven hundred and forty-six days of being invisible. At Rodel, who had worked himself into a breakdown for a company that didn't care.

"Rodel," Juan said.

*"Yes, boss?"*

"Save the 'True' report as a hidden attachment. We’re going to submit the 'Official' one for the audit."

The shadow hesitated. *"Boss?"*

"We're not strong enough for a war yet, Rodel," Juan said, his eyes on the flickering True Rank above Dennis’s head. "We’re going to level up first. We’re going to let Aldrich think he won. And then, we’re going to Raise the whole company."

Juan hit 'Send'.
