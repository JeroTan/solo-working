# Chapter 21 — The Global Invitation

*In the office, an invitation to the Global Office is either a coronation or an execution. And when you're a System Hybrid, you have to be ready for both.*

---

The week following the audit was a blur of high-level meetings, celebratory dinners, and a salary increase that finally allowed Juan dela Cruz to buy a decent suit and some salt for his eggs. 

He was still in Room 1409, but the door now had a frosted glass panel that read: **JUAN DELA CRUZ — GLOBAL TECHNICAL LEAD**.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               STATUS UPDATE              ║
║                                          ║
║  Current Rank: B (Global Lead)           ║
║  Status: [ ASCENDED ]                    ║
║  Current Shadows: 2/2                    ║
║  New Ability: [ HYBRID CONSCIOUSNESS ]   ║
║  (Allows 50% integration without pain)   ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Juan sat at his desk, staring at a silver-edged envelope that had been hand-delivered by a courier from Singapore. It didn't have a stamp. It had a wax seal in the shape of a gear. 

"Don't open it yet," Isabel Reyes said, leaning against his doorframe. She was still his 'Operational Consultant', but the tablet in her hand now contained more shadow-data than audit-data.

"Why not?" Juan asked. 

"Because once you open that, you're no longer just a Manila success story. You're a Global Asset. And Global Assets are managed differently."

Isabel walked into the room and closed the door. She looked at the silver envelope with a mix of awe and fear.

"I’ve been digging into Marcus Vane’s background," she whispered. "He doesn't exist in the Singapore corporate registry. He’s part of a group called 'The Founders'. They’re the ones who designed the gamification initiative that Pinnacle uses. They’re the ones who built the System."

Juan picked up the envelope. He felt a faint pulse of energy from the paper—the same energy he felt from his shadows.

"I know," Juan said. "Marcus told me. They’ve been looking for a 'Raise' in this region."

"Juan, if they built the System, they can delete the System. And they can delete the Workers."

"Then I’ll just have to make sure they can't delete me."

Juan broke the seal. 

Inside was a single card made of a dense, dark material that felt like carbon fiber. It wasn't a letter. It was a System Quest.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               MAIN QUEST: WORLD STAGE    ║
║                                          ║
║  Objective: Attend the 'Founders Summit' ║
║  in Singapore.                           ║
║  Reward: Rank Up to A, True History.     ║
║  Penalty: System Reset.                  ║
║                                          ║
║  [ ACCEPT ]      [ DECLINE ]             ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Juan looked at the [ ACCEPT ] button. He felt Rodel and Cynthia stir in his shadow. They were nervous. They remembered what it was like to be broken by the System. They didn't want to go back to the source.

"I have to go, Isabel," Juan said.

"I know. That’s why I’m going with you."

Juan looked up, surprised. "Gabriel authorized that?"

"Gabriel didn't authorize anything," Gabriel Soriano said, walking into the office. The CEO looked older, more tired than he had been during the audit. "I’m coming too. Project Phoenix is my legacy. And I’m not letting the Global Office take it without a fight."

```
║  STATUS: GABRIEL SORIANO                 ║
║  TRUE RANK: SSS                          ║
║  NEW AFFINITY: [ PROTECTOR ]             ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Juan looked at his two allies—the Auditor and the Arbiter. They were the highest-ranked humans he knew, but he knew that against 'The Founders', they were still just Rank F.

"Okay," Juan said, pressing the [ ACCEPT ] button on the card. 

The card dissolved into a cloud of blue pixels that merged with his workstation. 

**[ FLIGHT RESERVED. DESTINATION: SINGAPORE GLOBAL HUB. ]**

Juan stood up and straightened his new suit. He looked at his reflection in the glass. He didn't see an invisible worker anymore. He saw a man who was about to audit the auditors.

"Rodel," Juan whispered.

*"Yes, boss?"*

"Pack the Skyfall logs. All of them. We’re going to show the Founders exactly how their system is being used."

*"Ready, boss."*
