# Chapter 12 — The Fallout

*Victory in the boardroom is like a spark in a server room. It looks brilliant for a second, but if you don't manage the heat, it’ll trigger the fire suppressants and leave everyone in the dark.*

---

The elevator ride back to the 11th floor was the quietest thirty seconds of Juan dela Cruz's life. 

Aldrich Santos stood in the corner, his shadow—a jagged, pathetic thing now—flickering against the brass panels. He didn't look at Juan. He didn't look at Cynthia. He looked at the floor numbers as if they were the ticking seconds of his own execution.

Cynthia Ocampo, on the other hand, was vibrating with an energy she hadn't felt in years. Her 'Creative Drive' was back in the purple, and her **TRUE RANK: A+** was glowing so brightly it was almost distracting.

"Juan," she whispered as the doors opened. "We need to talk. In the breakroom. Now."

Juan nodded. He stepped out onto the 11th floor, and the System immediately updated his environment.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               ZONE UPDATE                ║
║                                          ║
║  Environment: THE 11TH FLOOR (CONTRAST)  ║
║  Status: [ REPUTATION GAINED ]           ║
║  Hazard: [ POLITICAL JEALOUSY ]          ║
║                                          ║
║  New Passive Skill:                      ║
║  [ THE UNTOUCHABLE ] (Lvl 1)             ║
║  - Executives will hesitate to fire you  ║
║    while Project Phoenix is active.      ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

The breakroom smelled of burnt coffee and anticipation. Most of the floor had already heard rumors of the "Boardroom Massacre." As Juan and Cynthia walked in, the usual chatter died down.

Cynthia grabbed two cups of water, handed one to Juan, and closed the door.

"What are you, Juan?" she asked, her voice low and direct. "Don't give me the 'documentation' line. I saw the deck. I saw the data. No one on the 11th floor, not even Rodel at his best, could have built that engine in three hours."

Juan took a sip of the water. He could feel the eyes of his shadows, Rodel and Cynthia, watching from the dark corners of the room.

"I have a different way of looking at the work, ma'am," Juan said. "I see the connections that most people ignore. And I have... assistants."

Cynthia leaned back against the counter. "Assistants. In a company that won't even buy us new pens. Right. Well, whatever you’re doing, it worked. Aldrich is finished. Victor Tan is already calling the CEO to demand your involvement in the implementation phase."

"And what about you, ma'am?"

"Me?" Cynthia laughed, a genuine, light sound this time. "I’m the lead architect for Phoenix. I’ve been promoted to Rank A. Officially."

```
║  STATUS: CYNTHIA OCAMPO                  ║
║  OFFICIAL RANK: A                        ║
║  TRUE RANK: A+                           ║
║  New Affinity: [ ALLY ]                  ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

"But be careful, Juan," she added, her face turning serious. "Aldrich might be unranked, but he has friends on the 14th floor. And they don't like anomalies. They like predictability."

Juan nodded. "I know. The System... the company prefers the Ferrari brochures."

As if on cue, a new notification appeared in Juan's vision. 

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║              ⚠ TARGET DETECTED ⚠             ║
║                                              ║
║  NAME: ISABEL REYES                          ║
║  TITLE: INTERNAL AUDIT SPECIALIST            ║
║  OFFICIAL RANK: A                            ║
║  TRUE RANK: S (INVESTIGATOR)                 ║
║                                              ║
║  STATUS: [ ANALYZING YOU ]                   ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Juan looked through the glass window of the breakroom. A woman was standing by the elevator, talking to Dennis. She was wearing a perfectly tailored charcoal suit and holding a tablet like it was a weapon. She didn't look like a manager. She looked like a predator that hunted other predators.

Isabel Reyes turned her head. For a split second, her gaze met Juan's through the glass. 

She didn't look away. She didn't smile. She just tapped a note into her tablet.

```
║  ALERT: DETECTION METER: 15% [|        ] ║
║  Isabel Reyes is now tracking your       ║
║  'Statistical Anomalies'.                ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

"Juan?" Cynthia asked, noticed his distraction. "Are you okay?"

"I'm fine," Juan said, turning back to her. "I just realized that the dungeon didn't end. It just changed floors."

He finished his water and walked out of the breakroom. He had a lot to do. He had to level up his shadows, manage the Phoenix implementation, and somehow stay invisible to a woman whose entire job was to see the things that shouldn't exist.

"Rodel," Juan whispered as he sat at his desk.

*"Yes, boss?"*

"We need a firewall. Not for the server. For me."
