# Chapter 23 — The Logic Counter-Attack

*The problem with building a perfect system is that you become part of it. And in any system, the only thing more dangerous than a virus is a developer who knows exactly where the backdoors are hidden.*

---

In the darkness of the Founders' Core, the silence was absolute. Gabriel and Isabel were frozen, their Ranks grayed out as the Global Reset command paralyzed their consciousness. 

Juan dela Cruz, however, was still standing. 

His eyes weren't just glowing; they were streaming data. The 'Purge' that Marcus Vane had triggered was hitting a wall—a firewall that didn't exist in Singapore, but was being broadcast from a greasy desk on the 11th floor in Manila.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               SKILL ACTIVE               ║
║                                          ║
║       [ THE MANILA BACKUP ]              ║
║                                          ║
║  Effect: Your consciousness is mirrored  ║
║  to Station 11-47. The Global Reset      ║
║  is being treated as 'Legacy Data'.      ║
║  Latency: 0.04ms                         ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

"You think you’re the only ones who can build a system?" Juan’s voice was distorted, sounding like a thousand voices speaking in unison. "I spent seven hundred and forty-six days being ignored. I spent that time documenting your mistakes. Every glitch, every corruption, every 'Loyalty Bonus' you ever authorized—it’s all in my notebook."

Marcus Vane stood up, his liquid crystal eyes flickering with alarm. "Impossible. We deleted the Skyfall logs."

"You deleted the files," Juan said, stepping toward the table. The white glass floor began to crack beneath his feet, blue static sparking in the fissures. "But you didn't delete the shadows. Rodel! Cynthia! Raise the Core!"

The shadows didn't just emerge from Juan’s feet. They surged out of the white walls, the glass ceiling, and the glowing floor itself. In Singapore, where the System was everywhere, the shadows were everywhere. 

Rodel didn't look like a tired analyst anymore. He was a giant of dark static, his hands the size of servers. Cynthia was a queen of violet fire, her presence so intense it began to override the room’s climate control.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               SHADOW EVOLUTION           ║
║                                          ║
║  RODEL (THE CORE ARCHITECT)              ║
║  CYNTHIA (THE SYSTEM QUEEN)              ║
║                                          ║
║  Rank: SSS (Shadow Form)                 ║
║  Ability: [ GLOBAL OVERRIDE ]            ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

The twelve Founders stood up. They weren't just executives; they were System Lords. They reached into the air and pulled out weapons made of gold code—blades of logic and shields of encryption.

"You're a bug, Juan dela Cruz," the woman with the gold eyes said. "And we are the Debuggers."

The battle didn't happen with swords and shields. it happened with logic. 

Cynthia launched a 'Presentation of Truth'—a massive burst of violet energy that showed every Founder the exact moment they had betrayed their own code. Marcus Vane’s liquid crystal eyes shattered as he was forced to process the four million pesos he had hidden in the *Aethelgard* project.

Rodel slammed his hands into the floor, his 'Data Forensics' skill turning into a weapon. He didn't just find the corruption; he used it as a ground for his own attack. The Founders' gold code began to turn black, infected by the very greed they had used to build their empire.

```
║  STAMINA: 1% [|         ]                ║
║  WARNING: CORE COLLAPSE IMMINENT         ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

Juan felt his heart stopping. He was pushing the System Hybrid skill too far. His Manila backup was starting to overheat. In the real world, his notebook on Station 11-47 was beginning to smoke.

"One last move," Juan gasped. 

He looked at the woman with the gold eyes. She was the only one left standing, her gold shield holding off Cynthia’s fire.

"Raise," Juan whispered.

He didn't target her. He targeted the city. 

He targeted every Rank-F worker in Singapore. Every delivery guy, every documentation associate, every person who was currently being 'Optimized' by the System. 

He gave them his Rank.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║               THE FINAL RAISE            ║
║                                          ║
║  Effect: Every worker in the Hub is now  ║
║  Rank B. The System cannot process       ║
║  this many leads simultaneously.         ║
║                                          ║
║  Result: [ GLOBAL SYSTEM CRASH ]         ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

The world went white. 

The gold-eyed woman’s shield shattered. Marcus Vane vanished into a cloud of pixels. The Founders' table collapsed into the glowing floor. 

The System didn't just stop. It rebooted.
